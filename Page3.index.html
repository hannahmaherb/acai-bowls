Menu

At Blissful Acai Bowls, we're dedicated to crafting the most delicious and nutritious acai bowls for you. Our menu is designed to satisfy a variety of tastes, from classic flavors to unique creations. Each bowl is made to order, ensuring you receive the freshest ingredients and a burst of natural flavors.

Classic Acai Bowls:

1. Pure Bliss Bowl:
Ingredients: Acai, banana, strawberries, granola, honey drizzle.
2. Tropical Sunrise Bowl:
Ingredients: Acai, pineapple, mango, coconut flakes, chia seeds, Nutella drizzle.
3. Berry Delight Bowl:
Ingredients: Acai, mixed berries, almond butter, hemp seeds, and a drizzle of honey.
4. Green Vitality Bowl:
Ingredients: Acai, spinach, kale, banana, almond milk, almond butter, and granola.

Specialty Acai Bowls:

1. Chocolate Lovers' Bowl:
Ingredients: Acai, cacao, banana, almond butter, chocolate chips, and a Nutella drizzle.
2. Peanut Butter Power Bowl:
Ingredients: Acai, peanut butter, banana, crushed peanuts, and a peanut butter drizzle.
3. Island Paradise Bowl:
Ingredients: Acai, coconut milk, dragon fruit, kiwi, blueberries, coconut flakes, and agave drizzle.
4. Protein-Packed Bowl:
Ingredients: Acai, protein powder, banana, strawberries, almond milk, granola, and almond butter.

Coffee Menu:


Expresso-Based Coffees:
1. Espresso - A single shot of intense, rich espresso.
2. Double Espresso - A double shot of espresso for extra energy.
3. Cappuccino - Espresso with steamed milk and a velvety foam top.
4. Latte - Espresso with a generous amount of steamed milk and a light frothy top.
5. Mocha - Espresso combined with creamy chocolate and steamed milk, topped with whipped cream and cocoa.

House Specialties:
1. Caramel Macchiato - Espresso, steamed milk, and caramel syrup, finished with a drizzle of caramel.
2. Vanilla Latte - A classic latte with a touch of sweet vanilla flavor.
3. Hazelnut Mocha - A mocha with a delightful hint of hazelnut, whipped cream, and a dusting of cocoa.
4. Iced Coffee - Chilled coffee served over ice, with the option to add syrup or milk of your choice.
